PacMen

The PacMen project was an absolute blast, representing one of my initial undertakings at MIT XPro. As a kid I loved Pacman games, I was particularly excited to dive into this venture. The objective of the project was to click on the "add Pacman" button as many times as you desired, and then click on "start game" to observe the Pacmen moving in diverse directions across the screen. An intriguing aspect of the project was that, upon clicking "start" multiple times, the Pacmen would accelerate, racing across the screen at an even faster pace. I urge you to give it a try! Bus Tracker

This exciting project I've been working on - a real-time bus tracker located near MIT. By simply right-clicking your mouse, selecting "Inspect," and clicking on "Console," you'll be able to view the bus times in real-time. Additionally, the project features a sleek black map, making the entire experience even more visually stunning. If you're interested, please feel free to head over to the "Projects" section of my GitHub page to view this gem firsthand!
